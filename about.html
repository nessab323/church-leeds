Our History

Christ Apostolic Church International was established with a divine mandate to spread the Gospel of Jesus Christ to all nations. Our journey began with a small group of dedicated believers who gathered regularly for prayer, fellowship, and the study of God's Word.

In 1937, Rev. James McKeown arrived in Asamankese as the first UK Pentecostal missionary in response to Anim's request for a resident missionary in Ghana. Anim and his followers strongly practiced divine healing, trusting God for miracles instead of medical treatment. McKeown later contracted malaria and was taken to Ridge Hospital, where he recovered and returned to continue his mission. However, disagreement arose over his acceptance of medical care, leading to a split in the movement. McKeown led the Apostolic Church of Gold Coast, later moving to Akroso and Winneba, while Peter Anim continued his group as the Christ Apostolic Church International.

Today, we continue to build on the foundation laid by our founding members, expanding our reach and deepening our commitment to serving God and humanity. Our history is a testament to God's faithfulness and the power of a community united in purpose.

Mission & Vision

Understanding our purpose and direction

Our Mission

To proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ to all nations, making disciples who will transform their communities through faith, love, and service to God and humanity. We are committed to reaching the lost, nurturing believers, and equipping them for effective ministry.

Our Vision

To be a dynamic church that raises spiritually mature believers who impact their generation and bring transformation to society through the power of the Holy Spirit. We envision a community where every member discovers their God-given purpose and walks in it fully.

Our Purpose

We exist to glorify God through worship, evangelism, discipleship, fellowship, and ministry. Every program, service, and activity is designed to fulfill these five purposes and help believers grow in their relationship with Christ.

Our Community

We are a family of believers from diverse backgrounds and cultures, united by our faith in Jesus Christ. Our community is characterized by love, acceptance, and mutual support as we journey together in our spiritual walk.

Our Beliefs

The foundational truths that guide our faith and practice

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God. It is the supreme authority in all matters of faith and conduct, providing guidance for every aspect of life.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone. His death and resurrection provide the only way for humanity to be reconciled to God.

Holy Spirit

We believe in the Holy Spirit who indwells, guides, and empowers believers for holy living and effective service. The Spirit distributes spiritual gifts for the edification of the Church.
