Welcome to Our Church

We are delighted that you have chosen to visit our website. Christ Apostolic Church International is a vibrant community of believers committed to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ and transforming lives through faith, love, and service.

Whether you are seeking a place to worship, looking for spiritual growth, or simply want to be part of a loving community, we warmly invite you to join us. Experience the presence of God, connect with others, and discover your purpose in Christ.

Our Mission

To proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ to all nations, making disciples who will transform their communities through faith, love, and service to God and humanity.

Our Vision

To be a dynamic church that raises spiritually mature believers who impact their generation and bring transformation to society through the power of the Holy Spirit.

Our Values

Faith, Integrity, Excellence, Compassion, Unity, and Service. These principles guide everything we do as a body of Christ.
