Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What time are your Sunday services?

Our Sunday worship service begins at 14:00 PM. If you arrive a little earlier, you're welcome to join us for a 10-15 minute pre-service prayer which typically begins at 1:45 .

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have a dedicated parking area for church members and visitors.

Do you have programs for children?

Absolutely! Our Children's Ministry provides engaging programs for children of all ages during our Sunday services. Your children will learn about God's love in a safe, fun environment.

How can I get involved in ministry?

We encourage every member to find their place of service. Visit our Ministries page to learn about the different opportunities available, or speak with one of our leaders after service.
